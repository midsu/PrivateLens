## PrivateLens

üî≠ currently working on

In our project,PrivateLens addresses the critical issue of privacy in the digital age, particularly within the realm of public videography. In an era dominated by vlogging and widespread camera use, the potential for breaching the privacy of unsuspecting individuals captured in video footage has become a significant concern. PrivateLens proposes a software solution designed to automatically detect and anonymize the faces of those who have not consented to be filmed, thereby safeguarding their privacy. This initiative harnesses techniques in face detection, recognition, and image processing, with Python serving as the programming language. Originating from a desire to harmonize the creative freedom inherent in content production with a deep respect for personal privacy, PrivateLens exemplifies the commitment to developing technological solutions that are both innovative and ethically grounded.
